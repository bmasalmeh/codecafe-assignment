About CodeCafe

CodeCafe opened in 2020 as a space where developers can work on their projects while enjoying quality coffee. We understand that coding requires focus, good internet, and great coffee. Whether you're working on a personal project, studying programming, or meeting with your team, CodeCafe provides the perfect environment for productivity.

What We Offer:

  • Premium coffee and beverages
  • High-speed wifi
  • Comfortable seating
  • Weekly coding meetups
  • Fresh pastries and snacks
